The Core Logic
When you push a new element into the queue:

Add it to the back: Like a normal queue (q.push(x)).
Rotate: Take every element that was already in the queue (before the new one) and move them to the back, one by one.
By the time you finish rotating, the element you just pushed will have "jumped" to the front of the line!

Let's trace push(30) where the Queue is currently [10, 20]
Step 1: this.q.push(30)
We add the new item to the back, like a normal queue.
Queue state: [10, 20, 30]

Step 2: The Rotation Loop (for loop runs 2 times)
We take the front item and move it to the back, over and over, until the new item (30) reaches the front.

Iteration 1:

shift() takes 10 out.

push(10) puts 10 at the back.

Queue state: [20, 30, 10]
