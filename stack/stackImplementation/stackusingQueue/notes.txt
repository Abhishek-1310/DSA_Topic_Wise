The Core Logic
When you push a new element into the queue:

Add it to the back: Like a normal queue (q.push(x)).
Rotate: Take every element that was already in the queue (before the new one) and move them to the back, one by one.
By the time you finish rotating, the element you just pushed will have "jumped" to the front of the line!

shift :- move element to left side and front element removed [5,10,30] shift() => [10,30]
