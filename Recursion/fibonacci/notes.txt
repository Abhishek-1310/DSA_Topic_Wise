F(0) = 0

to find Fibonacci number formula is F(n) = F(n-1) + F(n-2) 
0 1 2 3 4 5 6
0 1 1 2 3 5 8

F(0) = 0
F(1) = 1

F(2) = F(1) + F(0)
     = 1 + 0
     = 1
F(3) = F(2) + F(1)
     = 1 + 1
     = 2
