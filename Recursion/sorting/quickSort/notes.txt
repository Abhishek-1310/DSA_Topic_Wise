smaller in the left larger in the right

2) put pivot at its place how to do ! check below (with help of move smaller at left larger at right of pivot)

then with help of j find element smaller than pivot then swap value of i and j do until i cross j after don't swap

3) when smaller elemnt came left and larger at right   (smaller pivot larger)
how you split it where pivot is place that is partition through partition we split smaller and larger
