how to decide low:-
"Can one element alone make the answer impossible?" means:
Is there a single value in the array that, no matter what else you do, makes the answer invalid?

Ship packages — YES
weights = [3, 5, 8, 2], capacity = 4

take weight 8 alone →
no matter how you split the days,
no matter how you arrange other packages,
8 simply CANNOT fit on a ship of capacity 4
one element alone = game over
You can't fix it by changing days. You can't fix it by reordering. That one 8 makes capacity=4 permanently impossible.

Koko — NO
piles = [3, 5, 8, 2], speed = 1

take pile 8 alone →
speed 1 means it takes 8 hours for that pile
slow? yes
impossible? NO — just takes more hours
No single pile makes speed=1 impossible. It just affects how many hours you need. You can always eat any pile at speed 1, just slowly.

Bouquets — NO
bloomDay = [3, 5, 8, 2], day = 1

take flower with bloomDay=8 alone →
on day 1 it hasn't bloomed yet
impossible? NO — just means that flower isn't available yet
