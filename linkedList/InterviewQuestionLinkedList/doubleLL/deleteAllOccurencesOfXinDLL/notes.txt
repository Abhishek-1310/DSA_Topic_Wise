Delete all occurrences in a doubly linked list
You are given the head of a doubly Linked List and a key x . Your task is to delete all occurrences of the given 

key x if it is present and return the new DLL.

Examples:

Input: 2<->2<->10<->8<->4<->2<->5<->2, x = 2

Output:  10<->8<->4<->5

Explanation: 
All Occurences of 2 have been deleted.
